Privacy Policy

Your data, your rights

1. Introduction

At NEXUS AI ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible from godices.github.io, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI generation platform.

2. Local Data Storage

Currently, NEXUS AI operates entirely on the client-side. This means:

  • Account Information: Your name, email, and password are stored locally in your browser's Local Storage. They are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Generations: Your generated content history and credit balance are also stored locally.

If you clear your browser data, your account and credits will be lost. We are not responsible for data loss caused by local storage clearing.

3. Third-Party Advertising & CPA Offers

To provide free credits, we partner with third-party networks like OGAds and AdLumia Media. When you click "Start Task":

  • You will be redirected to a third-party locker or offer wall.
  • These third parties may collect data (IP address, device info) as per their own privacy policies.
  • We do not control the data collection practices of these external networks.
  • Time-locked verification is used to grant credits, but we do not receive personal data from the offers you complete.

4. Cookies

NEXUS AI itself does not use tracking cookies. However, our third-party advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to track offer completion and prevent fraud.

5. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by navigating to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account.
